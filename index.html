The English Countryside

Spoiler Alert: Not home of the english muffin...

History

  • Made in New York
  • 1870's
  • Samuel Bath Thomas
  • Original Name: "Toaster Crumpet"

Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sourdough starter
  • 1 cup milk
  • 4 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 tbspn salt
  • 3 tbspn butter

Instructions

  • The day before: prep sourdough starter:
    Mix 1/4 cup starter with 1/2 cup of warm water and 1/4 cup flour
  • After waiting for rise for 45 minutes combine the rest of the ingredients:
    milk, flour, salt and butter
  • Lay the dough out on a floured surface, and then start to make small 3" wide dough balls
  • Once you have your rounds, let them sit about an hour
  • Now flatten out your dough balls, and place them onto a cast Iron griddle, and let cook for around 10 minutes, checking the sides for brown-ness.
